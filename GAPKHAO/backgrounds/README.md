# Must-Try Dishes Background Images

## 📁 How to Add Your Background Image

### **Step 1: Add Your Image**
1. Save your Thai street market image as: `must-try-bg.jpg`
2. Place it in this `backgrounds/` folder
3. Recommended size: 1920x1080px or larger
4. Format: JPG or PNG

### **Step 2: The Image Will Automatically Load**
- The website will automatically use `backgrounds/must-try-bg.jpg` as the background
- No code changes needed!
- Just refresh your browser to see the changes

### **File Name Requirements**
- **Must be named:** `must-try-bg.jpg` (or `.png`)
- **Location:** `backgrounds/` folder
- **Size:** 1920x1080px minimum recommended

### **Example:**
```
GAPKHAO/
├── backgrounds/
│   └── must-try-bg.jpg  ← Place your image here
├── index.html
├── css/
└── ...
```

### **Image Specifications**
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Recommended Size:** 1920x1080px or larger
- **File Size:** Under 2MB for best performance
- **Content:** Thai street food market scenes work best

### **How It Works**
The CSS automatically looks for `backgrounds/must-try-bg.jpg`. When you add your image:
1. The background will be muted (less colorful)
2. A golden overlay will be applied
3. The 4 dish cards will appear on top
4. Parallax scrolling effect will work

### **Tips**
- Use high-quality images for best results
- Muted colors work better than bright colors
- Street market scenes are ideal
- Avoid images with too much text

### **Current Setup**
The website is configured to use: `backgrounds/must-try-bg.jpg`

Just add your image and refresh the page!
