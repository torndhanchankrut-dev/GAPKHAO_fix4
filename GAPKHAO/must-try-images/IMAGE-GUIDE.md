## 🎨 Image Specifications

### Recommended Settings:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 800x600px minimum (1200x900px recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 16:9
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, appetizing

### Photography Tips:
- ✅ Natural lighting or warm artificial light
- ✅ Close-up shots showing food details
- ✅ Clean, uncluttered background
- ✅ Vibrant colors that look appetizing
- ✅ Show garnishes and presentation
- ❌ Avoid dark or blurry images
- ❌ Avoid watermarks or text overlays

## 🎯 Where to Find Images

### Free Stock Photo Sites:
- Unsplash.com (search: "thai food", "pad thai", "som tam")
- Pexels.com (search: "thai cuisine", "street food")
- Pixabay.com (search: "thai dishes")

### Tips:
1. Search for authentic Thai food images
2. Look for images with good lighting and composition
3. Download high-resolution versions
4. Rename files to match the required names above
5. Place them in this folder
